Connect

Come visit our club socials to meet people and connect with other like-minded peers. ACM meetings are great places to foster friendships and unwind after class. We also provide food and beverage at each meeting.

Prepare

Attend our workshops to launch and prepare for your professional career. Our workshops cover interview tips, resumes, data structures, software engineering technologies, and other topics.

Learn

Come out to our meetings and learn something new everytime. With the technology sector becoming increasingly larger and competitive, it is our goal to familiarize you with emerging ideas and practices in technology and beyond.

Welcome to the Association for Computing Machinery

University of Georgia Chapter

The ACM Chapter at UGA is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering the growth of the technology community at UGA. Our mission is to help advance computing as a profession, major, and community through local events and meetings. Our events and meetings are open to all students regardless of area of study or experience.
